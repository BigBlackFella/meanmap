What is Meanmap.com about?

Meanmap.com is an interactive map for artisans all over the world. Mean Stack has become one of the biggest communities of fans, contributors and backers worldwide.

Why was Meanmap.com made?

Meanmap.com was made because since the release of Meanmap, there wasn´t any website for mapping mean stack developers, collecting resources and creating tutorials by users outside their blog / websites they own. So it was clear for me to create such a service. It was heavily inspired by laramap.com

How can i contribute to tutorials or resources?

Simply by signing up. All other procedures are clear described on the website.

Can i support this project?

Yes - you can! Serving this website does cost money. It´s built on services that are not free. If you wan´t to support Laramap.com you can simply drop me a line on the contactform or subscribe to a "Sponsoring-Plan". Becoming a partner is also possible.

Which tools and services are used in Meanmap.com?

Basically it´s built on the Mean Stack & Bootstrap. But there´s a bunch of services used for email, pushing events, payments and other sections.

  • Definitely Nodejs
  • Angularjs
  • Expressjs
  • MongoDB
  • Bootstrap
  • Google Maps
  • Pusher
  • Stripe
  • Gravatar
  • dploy.io
